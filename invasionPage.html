Invasion Game Trailer

Initially a first year, first semester project, this game trailer was made for a second year, first semester project in order to learn necessary skills for video editing. The challenge was to take our game from the first year and to make an exciting and enticing trailer to convince people to play our game. I wanted to make a product I know I would be proud of so I employed skills using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe After Effects instead of just using the normal Adobe Premiere titles in order to create this game trailer.

Invasion Game

Controls: A / D - Move Left and Right
Left Mouse Click - Fire Missile
W - Fire Triple Missiles
